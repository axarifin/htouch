"Wherever the art of medicine is loved, there is also a love of humanity." — Hippocrates

The Human Touch is an initiative by students at the Schulich School of Medicine & Dentistry to celebrate the talents of the School's students in visual arts and prose, and to promote the relationship between humanities and medicine.

Medical students in the 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018 graduating classes were invited to submit visual pieces of any medium or prose.
